Crystal Collector

Rules of the game:

  1. - You will be given a random numbe as a Goal.
  2. - By clicking on the crystal's name you will add specific amount of points to your current Total.
  3. - You accumulate wins by matching your current total to the Goal.
  4. - You lose if your total exceeds the Goal.
  5. - Value of crystals is hidden from you until you click on it.
  6. - For every new game the value of crystal will change.
